Savor the taste of home

Whether you're longing for timeless Filipino dishes or exploring bold local creations, this is where your culinary adventure begins. Relish the flavors of home and indulge in every bite asyou experience the true essence of Lutong Pinoy

MAIN DISHES

Pork Adobo

A classic Filipino dish, slow-cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and spices for a savory, tangy taste.

Lechon Belly

Crispy, golden-brown skin tender, flavorful meat make this roasted delicacy unforgettable.

Pork Sisig

Sizzling chopped pork with bold, tangy, and creamy flavors for an exciting taste experience.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Crispy Filipino spring rolls filled with seasoned pork and vegetables.

Chicken Inasal

Grilled chicken marinated in a special vinegar-based sauce with lemongrass.

Kare-Kare

A rich Filipino stew with oxtail, tripes, and vegetables in peanut sauce.


Desserts

Buko Pie

A sweet Filipino coconut pie with a buttery crust.

Leche Flan

A creamy caramel custard dessert that melts in your mouth.

Halo-Halo

A refreshing dessert with a mix of shaved ice, sweet beans, and fruits topped with leche flan and ube ice cream.

Turon

Sweet banana rolls with a caramelized sugar coating, sometimes with jackfruit.

Pastel

Soft, sweet bread filled with a variety of fillings such as custard or chocolate.

Cassava Cake

A cake made out of cassava filled with sweet milks.
